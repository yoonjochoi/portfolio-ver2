Interactive Scenery

2012

interactive digital sketch

Interactive Scenery is a interactive digital sketch made in Processing. You can interact by moving the mouse and keeping it in a certain area. If the mouse is on the upper side of the sketch the sun will come up, if you have the mouse below the mid section of the sketch the sun will set and night will come.

Software: Processing
