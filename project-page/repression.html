Repression

Paper Transfer on Cotton Cloth / 25x250(cm) each / 2009

Wearing stockings and pantyhose has always been a frustration. Although it makes me look slimmer and slick, the pressure on the waist is sometimes unbearable. I have worn the stocking over the head, struggling under and sometimes tearing it, expressing the irritation. The daily annoyance is expressed by repeating the image over and over again.
