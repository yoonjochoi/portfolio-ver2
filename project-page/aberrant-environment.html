Aberrant Environment

Paper / 60x50(m) / 2010 / Group Project

The main attempt is to draw interest by providing a scene that gives an awareness of difference. The repeated form of cylinders is not expected in the middle of the forest. However, by making over a thousand paper cylinders, each one having a different height, our group has given change to the site, providing the unexpected.
