The Listserve

2012

web, storytelling

Group project: Alvin Chang, Greg Dorsainville, Josh Begley, Yoonjo Choi, Zena Koo

The Listserve is a massive email list. Only one person per day can send an email to more than a million people around the world. This is an experiment around conversational spaces and storytelling.

Below is the animation for our site.

Below is a data visualization of the first month of launching the project to public. It can be viewed here.
The height of the dot is the total amount of subscribers we had each day. You can view the amount of subscribers and unsubscribers per day, which is the blue and red bar above and below each dot.The transparency of the dot is the amount of responses each email has received via email, twitter and facebook. You can also view the email that has been sent out by hovering over each dot.
